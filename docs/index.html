Artist Biography

Dolores Martinez is an architect turned artist. She completed her architectural

education in Argentina. She lived and worked in Argentina, Japan and various cities

in the United States before settling in Houston. This collection of experiences

enriched her life and developed a sensitivity for human connections. After

experiencing flooding during Hurricane Harvey she has put her energy in rebuilding

not only her home but also her personal journey. She merged her design

background and lived experiences into her artistic identity. She started her training at

the Glassell School of Art at the MFAH in 2021. She has been active in printmaking

since.
